DET

DET provides you easy way to manage your expenses or lending money by very effective way. You will get different options to manage this like to manage expenses you can simply make entry on daily basis or on date wise and you will get a basic structure of daily expenses. Not only this you can keep track on date wise or on monthly weekly or yearly basis. Also you can export report in excel or pd format. Same in case of lending money you can track how much you have gave to whom and you can keep track of this also the person will get track of this as well to home you have given

Expenses Tracker

Through expenses tracker you can keep track of money you invested or spend on daily basis, also you can make entry on date wise and keep record on daily, weekly, monthly or yearly basis. You can also filter by date as well. You can keep track of spent money through credit card, debit card, upi transection and many more. Also you can export record of this.

Lending Money Tracker

Sometimes it happen you lend money to someone and that forgot or you forgot or that deny with this so in this case you can loose your money but now not more. Through this tracker you can keep track that who you lent money, how much you lent and at which date you lent also the person will get record of this as well. In this you can take report in either excel or pdf.
